Register to attend OzCHI 2025

Registrations for OzCHI 2025 are now open! Click here to register for tickets on the HFESA website.

Full registration includes:

  • Morning/afternoon tea
  • Lunch
  • Entry to all main conference sessions
  • All social events (Welcome Drinks, Conference Dinner)

Day registration includes:

  • Morning/afternoon tea
  • Lunch
  • Entry to all main conference sessions on that day

Workshop registration, additional tickets for welcome drinks and conference dinner can be purchased separately by all conference attendees.
